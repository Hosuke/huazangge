## English
  
  Mencius further develops his theory of innate goodness through practical examples, including the proper observance of mourning rites. He argues that the sincere grief displayed during the Three Years' Mourning demonstrates humanity's inherent moral nature. When Duke Wan of T'ang instinctively felt compelled to observe proper mourning rituals despite political pressure, Mencius saw this as evidence that "the nature of man is good" and that moral cultivation through education can develop this innate goodness.

  ## 中文
  
  孟子通过实践案例进一步阐发性善论，包括合宜的丧礼实践。他认为三年之丧中表现的真诚哀痛证明了人性本善的道德本质。当滕文公不顾政治压力本能地要求实行正规丧礼时，孟子视此为"性善"的证据，说明通过[[教育]]的道德修养能够发展这种先天善性。

  ## 日本語
