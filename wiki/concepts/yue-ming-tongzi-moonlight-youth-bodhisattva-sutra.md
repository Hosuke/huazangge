## English

The **Moonlight Youth** (月明童子, Yuèmíng Tóngzǐ) serves as the primary interlocutor in the *Sutra of the Moonlight Bodhisattva* (佛說月明菩薩經). His name "Moonlight" (月明) is employed by the Buddha as a simile for his pure and undefiled mind—comparing his spiritual clarity to the brilliance of moonlight that illuminates without obstruction. The text describes him as possessing "clean and pure conduct" (清潔之行), which renders him worthy of the Buddhist teaching.

The Buddha addresses this youth specifically on matters concerning the bodhisattva path, particularly the four great vows that bodhisattvas should maintain both in household life and monastic life. The Moonlight Youth represents the ideal listener—someone with the receptivity and spiritual capacity to receive the profound teachings on [[dana-paramita-bodhisattva-giving|dāna (giving)]] and [[upaya-expedient-means-buddhism|skillful means]].

In the Buddhist cosmology presented in this sutra, the Moonlight Youth is not merely a historical figure but serves as a type of the awakened aspirant who can progress rapidly toward [[bodhisattva-path|bodhisattvahood]]. His presence in the narrative framework follows the traditional structure of Buddhist sutras where the Buddha teaches a receptive disciple, thereby instructing both the assembly present and future practitioners who will encounter this text.

The sutra concludes with the entire gathering receiving the teaching with joy, demonstrating the Moonlight Youth's role as the catalyst for this transmission of the dharma. His clean conduct and pure mind make him the ideal recipient of the four vows and the teachings on [[nirodha-samapatti|meditative absorption]] and spiritual development that the Buddha expounds.

## 中文

**月明童子**為《佛說月明菩薩經》的主要對話者，其名「月明」乃佛陀以此比喻其心地光明皎潔，如同月光普照而無障礙。經文記載此童子「有清潔之行」，心地純淨，堪受大法。

佛陀針對月明童子開示菩薩大士在家出家之四願修行法要，包括善權方便之德行、與善知識共會之道、財寶與一切人共有之願，以及以法施及飯食常樂修行之心。此四願涵蓋了菩薩道之根本行持，體現大乘佛教強調之布施、智慧與慈悲相結合之精神。

月明童子在經中象徵具有清淨根器之修行者，能速發無上正真道意，疾得[[bodhisattva-path|無上菩提]]。其形象代表具足信根之弟子，既能領受如來深法，又能生起大乘利他之心。經末云「佛說經已，莫不歡喜，作禮而去」，顯示月明童子與會眾皆得法益，印證此經之殊勝功德。

## 日本語

**月明童子**（げつみょうどうし）は『佛說月明菩薩經』における主要な対話者として登場する。その名「月明」は、仏陀がこの童子の心の清純さを月光に喩えたものであり、障害なく照らす月の光のようにその心の明亮さを象徴する。経典には「清潔之行」を持つと記され、心が清浄であり仏教の教えを受けるに値する存在として描かれている。

仏陀は月明童子に対して、在家・出家における四つの大誓願について開示される。方便善巧の徳を修め、善知識と常に出会い、財宝を一切の人と共有し、法施と飯食施によって常住快楽の修行を行うという内容である。これらの四願は在家・出家を通じて菩萨道（ぼさつどう）を実践する基本をなすものである。
