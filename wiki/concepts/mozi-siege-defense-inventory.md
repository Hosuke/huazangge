## English

Mozi's "Banners and Flags" chapter establishes comprehensive inventory standards for siege defense, mandating specific quantities of strategic materials that must be stockpiled within fortified cities.

### Required Material Reserves

The text prescribes systematic accumulation of fourteen essential categories:

1. **Stone** (石) - Building materials for fortifications
2. **Firewood** (樵薪) - Fuel for fires and cooking
3. **Rush grass** (菅茅) - Roofing and bedding materials
4. **Reed grass** (萑葦) - Wicker work and screens
5. **Wood** (木) - Construction timber
6. **Charcoal** (炭) - Fuel for fires and forges
7. **Sand** (沙) - Firefighting and construction
8. **Pine and cypress** (松柏) - Durable timber and incense
9. **Fleabane** (蓬艾) - Medicinal herbs and insect repellent
10. **Hemp and fat** (麻脂) - Lamp oil and rope materials
11. **Metal and iron** (金鐵) - Weapons and tools
12. **Grain** (粟米) - Food provisions
13. **Wells and stoves** (井竈) - Water access and cooking facilities
14. **Quality weapons** (五兵) - Military equipment

### Administrative Standards

The chapter requires:
- **Quality materials** (重質) - Priority for quality over quantity
- **Stable residency** (有居) - Resident specialists for each area
- **Weapon-specific banners** (各旗) - Color-coded identification for each weapon type
- **Differentiated command** (節各有辨) - Clear chain of command
- **Legal standards** (法令各有貞) - Established regulations
- **Authority levels** (輕重分數) - Graduated decision-making powers
- **Road supervisors** (道路者有經) - Infrastructure maintenance personnel

## 中文

《墨子·旗幟》篇詳盡規定守城物資儲備標準，要求城中預備十四類戰略物資，以確保長期圍城作戰之持久力。

### 十四類物資儲備

墨子制定了系統化的物資儲備清單：

1. **石**：城防建材
2. **樵薪**：薪柴燃料
3. **菅茅**：葦草墊葢
4. **萑葦**：編織屏障材料
5. **木**：建築木材
6. **炭**：冶煉與取暖燃料
7. **沙**：滅火與建築用材
8. **松柏**：耐久木材與祭祀用品
9. **蓬艾**：藥草與驅蟲材料
10. **麻脂**：照明油脂與繩索原料
11. **金鐵**：兵器與工具
12. **粟米**：糧食儲備
13. **井竈**：水源與炊事設施
14. **五兵**：各類兵器

### 管理制度

物資儲備之法強調：
- **重質有居**：重視質量，專人保管
- **各有旗**：兵器各有識別旗幟
- **節各有辨**：指揮層級分明
- **法令各有貞**：法令規章明確
- **輕重分數各有請**：物資調配有相應權限
- **道路者有經**：道路維護專人負責

物資備蓄には以下の管理原則がある：
