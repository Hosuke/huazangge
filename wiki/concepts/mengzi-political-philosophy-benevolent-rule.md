## English

### Mencius's Political Philosophy of Benevolent Rule

Mencius's political philosophy centers on the concept of "Wangdao" (王道), or benevolent rule, which he contrasts with "Badao" (霸道), coercive rule based on force and manipulation. This fundamental distinction forms the basis of his critique of contemporary rulers and his prescription for ideal governance.

#### The Foundation of Political Legitimacy

For Mencius, political legitimacy derives exclusively from moral virtue. A ruler who governs through moral example (德治) rather than coercion commands genuine loyalty from his people. The famous exchange with King Liang Hui illustrates this principle: when asked why the king desires wealth and power, Mencius redirects the conversation toward the welfare of the people, arguing that true royal ambition should focus on ensuring the people's material security and moral development.

Mencius systematizes this principle through the concept of the "Mandate of Heaven" (天命), arguing that Heaven's favor depends on the ruler's moral performance. A ruler who fails to govern justly loses Heaven's mandate, and rebellion becomes justified. This position provides the theoretical foundation for the right of resistance against tyrannical rule.

#### The Relationship Between Virtue and Effective Governance

Mencius argues that virtue and effective governance are not merely compatible but necessarily connected. A ruler who cultivates personal virtue naturally attracts worthy ministers and establishes social harmony. This relationship operates through the mechanism of resonance (感應): moral influence spreads naturally from the ruler to the people, creating a well-ordered society without excessive legal intervention.

The ideal state, as Mencius describes it, combines agriculture with education. By ensuring the people's material welfare through proper economic policies and simultaneously cultivating their moral nature through education, the sage-ruler achieves the transformation of society through moral influence rather than coercion.

#### Practical Implications for Governance

Mencius provides concrete policy recommendations for benevolent governance:
- Reduction of excessive taxation
- Punishments that educate rather than merely punish
- Equal distribution of land
- Investment in education and moral cultivation
- Selection of officials based on moral merit rather than birth

These practical concerns demonstrate that Mencius's political philosophy is not merely idealistic but engages seriously with the problems of actual governance. His dialogues with rulers such as King Liang Hui, King Xuan of Qi, and the Duke of Teng show him addressing specific political situations while maintaining his commitment to moral principles.

The influence of Mencius's political philosophy extended throughout Chinese history. His arguments for the primacy of moral over hereditary legitimacy influenced the development of [[confucian-political-philosophy]] and provided intellectual justification for dynasty changes. His emphasis on the welfare of the common people and the right to resist tyranny made him a significant figure in later Chinese political thought.

## 中文

### 孟子王道政治思想

孟子政治哲學以「王道」為核心概念，與以武力征服為基礎之「霸道」形成根本對立。此區分構成孟子批判當世君主及提出理想政治之理論基礎。

#### 政治合法性之根基

孟子主張政治合法性唯賴道德德行。統治者以德服人而非以力制人，方能獲得百姓真心歸附。孟子與梁惠王之著名對話充分說明此原則：梁惠王問何以致富強，孟子不言功利之術，而先論百姓之生計疾苦。孟子認為，真王之志當在於保民而王，使民養生喪死無憾。

孟子進一步以「天命」觀念系統化此原則，主張天命之歸予以道德表現為轉移。無道之君喪失天命，起義反抗遂具正當性。此論為臣民反抗暴政提供理論根據，在儒家政治思想中具有重要地位。

#### 德治與治效之關係

孟子認為道德與治效不僅相容，而且必然關聯。以道德修養己身，自然感召賢才，社會和諧亦隨之而生。此作用透過「感應」機制運行：道德感化自君王流布百姓，形成不依賴嚴刑峻法之良好秩序。

孟子理想之政治形態，兼具農業與教育。通过合理經濟政策確保百姓物質福利，同時以教育培養其道德本性，聖王以感化而非強制達成社會改造。此理想為後世「德治」思想之淵源。

#### 治國實務之主張

孟子提出具體之治國方針：
- 薄賦斂以減輕百姓負擔
- 刑罰以教育為目的
- 平均地權以抑兼併
- 興學重教以育人才
- 選賢與能而非以血統用人

此等實際主張顯示孟子政治哲學非徒托空言，而是認真面對治國實務。其與梁惠王、齊宣王、滕文公等君主之對話，無不針對具體政治處境，同時堅持道德原則。

#### 歷史影響

孟子王道政治思想影響深遠。其以道德而非血統論政治合法性，為歷代易姓革命提供思想依據。其重視民生、主張抵抗暴政之說，成為後世批判專制之重要思想資源。

## 日本語

### 孟子の王道政治思想

孟子の政治哲学は「王道」に中心を置き、武力による支配に基づく「霸道」と根本的に対比される。この基本的な区別は、現代の統治者への批判と理想的な統治の処方の基盤を形成する。

#### 政治的正統性の基盤

孟子にとって、政治的正統性はもっぱら道徳的美徳に由来する。強制ではなく道徳的模範（徳治）によって統治する統治者は、人民の真の忠誠を得る。『梁恵王』との有名な対話はこの原則を例証する：王が富と権力を欲する理由を聞かれたとき、孟子」は会話を人民の福祉に向け、真の王の野望は人民的物質的保障と道徳的発展を確保することにあると主張する。

孟子」はこの原則を「天命」の概念を通じて体系化し、天の支持は統治者の道徳的パフォーマンスに依存すると主張する。正しく統治しない統治者は天の支持を失い、反乱が正当化される。この立場は専制的な支配に対する抵抗権の理論的基盤を提供する。

#### 徳と効果的な統治の関係

孟子」は徳と効果的な統治が単に両立するものではなく必然的に結びついていると主張する。個人的な美徳を修養する統治者は自然に有能な臣下を引き寄せ、社会的和諧を確立する
