## English

The Mozi chapter "Yingdi Ci" (迎敵祠, Welcoming Enemy Rituals) represents a significant intersection of ritual, cosmology, and military strategy in Warring States period China. This text outlines systematic preparations for defending against enemy invasions by establishing directional ritual protocols that align military defense with cosmic and spiritual principles.

### Directional Ritual System

The text establishes a comprehensive four-directional framework for enemy reception:

**Eastern Enemy Arrival:**
- Eastern altar: 8 chi (尺) height, chamber depth of 8 chi
- Eight ritual officiants aged 80 years
- Green (青) flag as the primary banner
- Green deity image 8 chi tall
- Eight crossbows, each firing eight shots
- Commander's attire must be entirely green
- Sacrifice: chicken (雞)

**Southern Enemy Arrival:**
- Southern altar: 7 chi height, chamber depth of 7 chi
- Seven ritual officiants aged 70 years
- Red (赤) flag as the primary banner
- Red deity image 7 chi tall
- Seven crossbows, each firing seven shots
- Commander's attire must be entirely red
- Sacrifice: dog (狗)

**Western Enemy Arrival:**
- Western altar: 9 chi height, chamber depth of 9 chi
- Nine ritual officiants aged 90 years
- White (白) flag as the primary banner
- White deity image 9 chi tall
- Nine crossbows, each firing nine shots
- Commander's attire must be entirely white
- Sacrifice: sheep (羊)

**Northern Enemy Arrival:**
- Northern altar: 6 chi height, chamber depth of 6 chi
- Six ritual officiants aged 60 years
- Black (黑) flag as the primary banner
- Black deity image 6 chi tall
- Six crossbows, each firing six shots
- Commander's attire must be entirely black
- Sacrifice: pig (彘)

### Numerological Symbolism

The text demonstrates a sophisticated numerological system correlating:
- **Altitude**: Directional height inversely proportional to symbolic importance (East 8, South 7, West 9, North 6)
- **Age of officiants**: Directly matches the altar height (80, 70, 90, 60)
- **Crossbow quantities**: Equal to directional numbers
- **Shot counts**: Matching crossbow numbers

This suggests a cosmological worldview where human ritual parameters must conform to cosmic numerical harmonies.

### Spiritual Preparations

The text mentions summoning spirit mediums (靈巫) to pray at major external shrines (外宅諸名大祠), providing sacrificial animals for these rituals (給禱牲).

## 中文

《墨子·迎敵祠》是先秦兵陰陽家的重要文獻，系統闡述了依據敵軍來犯方向而設置的祭祀儀典與軍事準備。本文將宇宙方位觀、數字象徵與軍事防御融為一體，展現了戰國時期「天人合一」軍事思想之精髓。

### 四向祭祀體系

**東方來敵：**
- 東壇高八尺，堂深八尺
- 八年八十者八人主祭
- 青旗為主祭旗幟，青神像高八尺
- 弩八具，各發八矢而止
- 將服必青，以雞為牲

**南方來敵：**
- 南壇高七尺，堂深七尺
- 年七十者七人主祭
- 赤旗為主祭旗幟，赤神像高七尺
- 弩七具，各發七矢而止
- 將服必赤，以狗為牲

**西方來敵：**
- 西壇高九尺，堂深九尺
- 年九十者九人主祭
- 白旗為主祭旗幟，素神像高九尺
- 弩九具，各發九矢而止
- 將服必白，以羊為牲

**北方來敵：**
- 北壇高六尺，堂深六尺
- 年六十者六人主祭
- 黑旗為主祭旗幟，黑神像高六尺
- 弩六具，各發六矢而止
- 將服必黑，以彘為牲

### 數字象徵意涵

本篇構築精密數理系統：
- **高度**：東八、南七、西九、北六——與方位重要性相呼應
- **祭司年齡**：與祭壇高度完全對應
- **弩矢數量**：與方位數字一致
- **發射次數**：等於弩具數量

此系統反映戰國時期「數通天人」的宇宙觀念，強調人事必須契合天數方能致勝。

### 祭祀準備

另遣靈巫於外宅大祠祈禱，備牲以供祭祀之用。此為兵陰陽家「祀戎並重」思想之體現。

## 日本語

### 数霊 symbolism

本文は精緻な数霊体系を示す：
- **高さ**：東8・南7・西9・北6——方位の重要性と対応
- **祭官の年齢**：壇の高さと完全一致
- **弩の数**：方位の数字に等しい
- **発射回数**：弩の数に一致

この体系は、人間事象が天の数字に合わせねばならないという宇宙観を反映している。
