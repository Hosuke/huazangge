## English

Benevolent government is a central concept in the teachings of Mencius, emphasizing that a ruler's morality directly influences the morality of the subjects. Mencius asserted that "If the ruler be benevolent, all will be benevolent; if the ruler be righteous, all will be righteous." This highlights that the ethical disposition of leaders sets the moral tone for the entire society. 

In Mencius’ view, when ministers successfully advise their rulers and blessings descend upon the people, it reflects a benevolent governance structure. However, when a ruler disregards the counsel of their ministers, the state is negatively affected, leading to chaos and unrest. 

A notable example in Mencius' teaching is the idea of "thrice-repeated displays of consideration," where a ruler provides due respect to a minister who has served well, ensuring a harmonious relationship. Mencius criticized rulers who treat their ministers poorly by seizing their possessions or acting punitively when those ministers depart, suggesting that such behavior is akin to that of a robber or enemy, ultimately eroding governance and morality in society.

Thus, the quality of governance is intrinsically linked to the virtues of the ruler, impacting the populace's ethical conduct.

## 中文

仁政是孟子教义中的核心概念，强调统治者的道德直接影响臣民的道德。孟子曾言，“君子仁，皆仁；君子义，皆义。” 这突显出领导者的伦理观为整个社会设定了道德基调。

在孟子看来，当大臣能够成功地向统治者建议并使民众获得福祉时，这反映出仁政的结构。然而，当统治者不重视大臣的劝谏时，国家就会受到负面影响，导致混乱和骚动。

孟子教导中的一个显著例子是“三次表示关心”，统治者对曾经尽职的大臣给予应有的尊重，从而建立和谐的关系。孟子批评那些对大臣不公的统治者，因他们的行为像强盗或敌人，最终侵蚀了国家治理和社会道德。

因此，治理的质量与统治者的美德息息相关，并且影响着民众的伦理行为。

## English

### Confucian Benevolent Governance

The concept of [[benevolent-government]] emerges strongly in Chapter Xian Wen, where Confucius discusses the characteristics of virtuous rule and the qualities needed for effective governance.

#### Virtue Over Force

Confucius praises those who value virtue over military prowess:
> "羿善射，奡盪舟，俱不得其死然；禹稷躬稼，而有天下。"
> "Yi was skilled in archery, Ao was skilled in naval warfare, yet neither died a natural death; Yu and Ji personally engaged in agriculture and gained the empire."

This illustrates the Confucian preference for [[benevolent-government]] based on agricultural virtue over rule through military force.

#### The Power of Virtuous Example

Confucius emphasizes that proper conduct by rulers facilitates governance:
> "上好禮，則民易使也。"
> "When superiors love ritual, the people are easily directed."

This connects to the broader Confucian concept that [[benevolent-government]] begins with the moral cultivation of rulers.

#### Historical Examples of Governance

Confucius evaluates historical figures based on their governance:
> "晉文公譎而不正，齊桓公正而不譎。"
> "Duke Wen of Jin was crafty but not upright; Duke Huan of Qi was upright but not crafty."

The case of Guan Zhong demonstrates Confucius's pragmatic approach to [[benevolent-government]], valuing results that benefit the people.

## 中文

### 儒家仁政思想

《论语·宪问》篇中[[benevolent-government|仁政]]概念显著呈现，孔子讨论了德治的特征与有效治理所需的品质。

#### 德性胜于武力

孔子赞扬重德轻武之人：
> "羿善射，奡盪舟，俱不得其死然；禹稷躬稼，而有天下。"

这说明了儒家偏好基于农耕德性的[[benevolent-government|仁政]]，而非通过军事力量的统治。

#### 德性榜样的力量

孔子强调统治者正当行为促进治理：
> "上好禮，則民易使也。"

这与更广泛的儒家概念相连，即[[benevolent-government|仁政]]始于统治者的道德修养。

#### 治理的历史例证

孔子根据治理评价历史人物：
> "晉文公譎而不正，齊桓公正而不譎。"

管仲的案例展示了孔子对[[benevolent-government|仁政]]的务实态度，重视造福人民的结果。

### 儒家の仁政

#### 力より徳

孔子は軍事力より徳を重視する者を称賛する：

これは、軍事力による支配よりも農業的徳性に基づく[[benevolent-government|仁政]]への儒家的選好を示している。

孔子は統治者の適切な行為が統治を促進すると強調する：
> "上好禮，則民易使也。"

これは、[[benevolent-government|仁政]]が統治者の道徳的修養から始まるというより広範な儒家的概念につながる。

#### 統治の歴史的例

> "晉文公譎而不正，齊桓公正而不譎。"

管仲の事例は、人々に利益をもたらす結果を重視する孔子の[[benevolent-government|仁政]]への実用的アプローチを示している。
