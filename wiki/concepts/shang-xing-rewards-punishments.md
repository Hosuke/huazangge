## English

**Unified Rewards and Punishment (賞刑)** refers to the three core policies—unified rewards (壹賞), unified punishment (壹刑), and unified education (壹教)—presented in Chapter 17 of *The Book of Lord Shang*. Together, these policies formed the foundation of Shang Yang's Legalist governance system.

### The Three Unifications

#### 1. Unified Rewards (壹賞)

The principle of 壹賞 means that all rewards, including profits, official ranks, and titles, must come from military service. No other path to wealth or status exists. This creates a system where:

- All people, regardless of birth or status, must contribute militarily
- Rewards are distributed exclusively through martial achievement
- Even former nobles and commoners alike must earn their positions through battlefield performance

The text states: "The so-called unified reward means that profits, official ranks, and titles all stem from military service, with no differentiated treatment."

#### 2. Unified Punishment (壹刑)

The principle of 壹刑 means that punishment knows no hierarchy. From ministers and generals to commoners, all are subject to the same laws. The text explicitly states:

- "From prime ministers and generals down to大夫 (officers) and commoners, those who violate the king's orders, violate national prohibitions, or disturb the established system shall be executed without pardon"
- Even meritorious past service does not reduce punishment for later crimes
- Even good deeds in the past do not excuse violations in the present

This is expressed as: "There is no level of status exempt from punishment."

#### 3. Unified Education (壹教)

The principle of 壹教 means that education focuses solely on military valor and loyalty to the state. Traditional literacies such as "broad learning, eloquent debate, integrity, ritual, music, and cultural refinement" are explicitly discouraged. The text states:

- Those who are "well-informed, eloquent, honest, graceful, or socially prominent" cannot use these talents to escape punishment or gain wealth
- The only path to富贵 (wealth and honor) is through military service
- Fathers encourage sons, older brothers encourage younger brothers, wives encourage husbands—all to go to war

### The Goal: Eliminating Distinctions

Shang Yang's ultimate aim was to eliminate the distinctions that divided society:

- "Through unified rewards, there will be no enemy under heaven"
- "Through unified punishment, orders will be executed"
- "Through unified education, the common people will follow the ruler"

The text describes the ideal outcome: "Through unified education, even without explicit rewards and punishments, the people will be good. This is called reaching the point of no rewards, no punishments."

### Historical Examples

The chapter cites historical examples:
- **Tang of Shang**: Granted land to officers and soldiers after defeating the Xia
- **King Wu of Zhou**: After defeating Zhou, ceased military activities and promoted cultural refinement
- **Duke Huan of Qi**: Executed颠颉 for being late to battle, establishing discipline

### Significance

The 賞刑 doctrine represents Shang Yang's complete rejection of Confucian moral governance. By unifying rewards, punishments, and education around military objectives, he created a totalitarian system that subordinated all social activities to state power. This became the ideological foundation for Qin's transformation from a peripheral state to the unifier of China.

## 中文

**賞刑**是《商君書》第十七章所載商鞅的核心政治理念，包括「壹賞」（統一賞賜）、「壹刑」（統一刑罰）、「壹教」（統一教化）三大原則。這三者共同構成了法家治國的完整體系。

### 三統一原則

#### 壹賞

「壹賞」原則意味著一切賞賜——無論是利祿、官爵——皆出於軍事功績，别無他途。原文云：「所謂壹賞者，利祿官爵，摶出於兵，無有異施也。」此原則要求：

- 不論貴賤智愚，皆須以軍功取賞
- 賞賜的唯一來源是戰場斬獲
- 即使是舊貴族亦須通過軍功獲得地位

#### 壹刑

「壹刑」原則意味著刑罰面前人人平等。原文明言：「所謂壹刑者，刑無等級。自卿相將軍以至大夫庶人，有不從王令，犯國禁，亂上制者，罪死不赦。」具體而言：

- 有功於前，有敗於後，不為損刑
- 有善於前，有過於後，不為虧法
- 忠臣孝子有過，必以其數斷

#### 壹教

「壹教」原則意味著教育目標的統一，即以耕戰為本。原文明確排斥傳統文教：「博聞辯慧，信廉禮樂，修行群黨，任譽清瘺，不可以富貴，不可以評刑，不可獨立私議以陳其上。」其核心要義：

- 富貴之門，要在戰而已矣
- 父遺其子，兄遺其弟，妻遺其夫，皆曰：「不得，無返」

### 最終目標：無賞無刑

商鞅的最終理想是通過三統一達到「明賞之猶，至於無賞也；明刑之尤，至於無刑也；明教之尤，至於無教也」的境界——即賞刑教化深入人心，不再需要外在的賞刑手段。

### 歷史例證

本章援引歷史事例為證：
- 湯伐桀，斬首封侯
- 周武王偃武修文
- 晉文公斬顛頡以殉

### 歷史意義

賞刑統一學說是商鞅對儒家德治思想的徹底否定。通過將賞、刑、教三者統一於軍事目標，商鞅創造了一個將所有社會活動都隸屬於國家權力的極權體制。這成為秦國從邊陲小國崛起為統一六國的強國的思想基礎。

## 日本語

**賞刑**（しょうぎょう）は『商君書』第十七章に記された商鞅の核心政治理念であり、「壹賞」（統一賞賜）、「壹刑」（統一刑罰）、「壹教」（統一教化）の三原則を含む。これらは法家統治の完全な体系を構成する。

### 三つの統一原則

#### 壹賞

「壹賞」の原則とは、すべての賞金・利禄・官爵が軍事功績から生じることを意味する。原文は「所謂壹賞者，利祿官爵，摶出於兵，無有異施也」と述べる。この原則は以下を要求する：
- 貴賤智愚に関わらず、すべて軍功によって賞を得る
- 賞の唯一の出処は戦場での武功
- 旧貴族も軍功によって地位を得なければならない

#### 壹刑

「壹刑」の原則とは、刑罰の前に人人平等であることを意味する。原文は明確に記載する：「所謂壹刑者，刑無等級。自卿相將軍以至大夫庶人，有不從王令，犯國禁，亂上制者，罪死不赦。具体的には：
- 前日の功績は後の罪を軽減しない
- 前日の善行は現在の過ちを帳消しにしない
- 忠臣孝子이라도過ちがあれば必ず罰する

#### 壹教

「壹教」の原則とは、教育目標を耕戦（農業と軍事）に統一することを意味する。原文は伝統的な文教を明確に排斥する：「博聞辯慧，信廉禮樂...不可以富貴，不可以評刑」。核心は：
- 富貴への道は軍事にある
- 父は子に、兄は弟に、妻は夫に、「戦場で会おう」と告げる

### 最終目標：無賞無刑

商鞅の最終理想は三統一を通じて「明賞之猶，至於無賞也；明刑之尤，至於無刑也明教之尤，至於無教也」の境地を達成することである。
