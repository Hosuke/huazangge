## English

The distinction between **the Way of the True King (王道, Wángdào)** and **hegemony (霸道, Bàdào)** represents one of the central themes in Mencius's political teachings. In his dialogues with rulers of the Warring States period, Mencius consistently argued that true legitimacy in governance derives from moral virtue, not military might or political manipulation.

Mencius presents this distinction most clearly in his conversation with King Xuan of Qi regarding the achievements of Duke Huan of Qi and Duke Wen of Jin—historical figures often celebrated as paradigmatic hegemonic rulers. While acknowledging their accomplishments, Mencius distinguishes himself from the Confucian tradition that would discuss such rulers, suggesting that **true kinghood (王)** requires something qualitatively different from the successful but ultimately force-dependent hegemony of the Five Hegemons.

The criteria for true kinghood, according to Mencius, include: genuine concern for the people's welfare (**[[benevolent-government-ren-zheng|仁政]]**), the ability to attract talented officials and willing subjects from all directions, and ultimately, the capacity to unify the realm through moral attraction rather than military conquest. A ruler who achieves this status truly "nourishes the people" and becomes, in Mencius's famous phrase, the "parent of the people" (民之父母).

Mencius illustrates the consequences of hegemonic versus kingly rule through the **[[mengzi-losing-original-heart|cultivation of the heart-mind]]** and the natural tendencies of water flowing downward. Just as water naturally moves downward, the people naturally submit to a benevolent ruler; they cannot be forced into lasting allegiance through violence alone.

## 中文

**王道與霸道**的區別是孟子政治思想的核心主題。孟子認為，真正的治國合法性來自道德仁義，而非軍事力量或政治權謀。

在與梁惠王的對話中，孟子明確區分了「王」與「霸」的不同。他指出：「萬乘之國，弒其君者，必千乘之家；千乘之國，弒其君者，必百乘之家。」這種以下篡上的現象，正是霸道政治的必然結果——因為上下皆以利害相處，國必危矣。

真正王道的標準包括：以民為本、實行仁政、能使天下仕者皆欲立於其朝、耕者皆欲耕於其野。當統治者實行仁政時，「天下之民皆引領而望之矣」，如「水之就下」，無法阻擋。

孟子告誡梁惠王：「地方百里，而可以王」，關鍵在於「施仁政於民」。反之，若不行仁政而只追求霸道，則雖可得一時之利，終必敗亡。

## 日本語

**王道と霸道**の区別は、孟子の政治思想における中心的主題です。孟子は、真の治世の正当性は道徳的徳から生じるものであり、軍事力やお政治的操作によるものではないと主張しました。

梁恵王との対話において、孟子は明確に「王」と「霸」の違いを区別しました。「万乗の国，其の君を弑す者は必ず千乗の家に由る；千乗の国，其の君を弑す者は必ず百乗の家に由る」と指摘するように、利害だけで結びついた関係は国家を危険に導くのです。

真の王道を実現するための基準には、民衆の福祉への真摯な関心、仁政の実行、有能な官吏と民心を引きつける能力などが含まれます。孟子が有名に述べたように、こうして統治する者は「民の父母」となります。
