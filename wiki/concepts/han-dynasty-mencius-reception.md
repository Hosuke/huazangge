## English

The Han dynasty (206 BC-220 AD) marked the critical period of recognition and preservation for the [[Mencius|Works of Mencius]]. According to historical records, Mencius's writings were officially registered in the imperial catalogue compiled by Lew Hin around 1 AD, listed as "The Works of Mencius, in eleven Books."

Earlier evidence comes from historian Sze-ma Ts'een (c. 145-86 BC), who in his "Historical Records" documented that Mencius, after withdrawing from public life, "with his disciples, Wan Chang and others, prefaced the Sho and the Shoo, unfolded the views of Confucius, and made 'The Works of Mencius, in seven Books.'"

The preservation narrative includes Chaou K'e's claim that during the Qin dynasty's infamous burning of books and burial of scholars, Mencius's works survived because they were "included under the common name of 'Philosophical,' and so the tablets containing them escaped destruction." While historian Ma Twan-lin questioned this account, supporting evidence comes from Han Ying and Tung Chung-shoo's quotations from complete Mencius texts during early Han.

During Emperor Heaou-wën's reign (c. 154 BC), Mencius achieved temporary official status when "the Lun-yu, the Heaou-king, Mencius, and the Urh-ya were all put under the care of a Board of Great Scholars," though this arrangement lasted less than half a century before focus returned exclusively to the Five Classics.

## 中文

汉代（公元前206年-公元220年）是《孟子》获得认可与保存的关键时期。根据历史记载，刘歆约公元元年编纂的皇家目录中正式收录了"《孟子》十一篇"。

更早的证据来自史学家司马迁（约公元前145-86年），他在《史记》中记载孟子"退而与万章之徒序《诗》《书》，述仲尼之意，作《孟子》七篇"。

关于《孟子》的保存，赵岐提出在秦朝焚书坑儒时，孟子著作因"被列为诸子之类而得以免于毁灭"。尽管马端临对此说提出质疑，但早期汉代的韩婴、董仲舒引用《孟子》完整文本提供了佐证。

汉孝文帝时期（约公元前154年），《孟子》曾获得官方地位，"《论语》、《孝经》、《孟子》、《尔雅》皆设博士官"，但这种制度维持不到半个世纪，学术重点重新回到五经。

## 日本語

漢代（紀元前206年-紀元220年）は『孟子』が認知と保存を受けた重要な時期であった。歴史的記録によると、劉歆が紀元元年頃に編纂した皇室目録に「孟子十一篇」が正式に登録されている。

より早期の証拠としては、歴史家司馬遷（紀元前145年頃-86年）が『史記』において、孟子が「退いて万章の徒と詩書を序し、仲尼の意を述べて、孟子七篇を作る」と記録している。

『孟子』の保存に関する叙述では、趙岐が秦代の焚書坑儒において、孟子の著作が「諸子に列せられて destruction を免れた」と主張している。馬端臨がこの説に疑問を呈しているものの、漢代初期の韓嬰や董仲舒による完全な孟子テキストの引用が裏付けを提供している。

漢の孝文帝の治世（紀元前154年頃）には、『孟子』は一時的に公的地位を獲得し、「論語、孝経、孟子、爾雅は皆博士官の管理下に置かれた」が、この制度は半世紀も経ずして五経のみに焦点が戻された。

  ## English
  
  The textual history of Mencius reveals important details about its compilation and early reception. According to Sze-ma Ts'een's records, the original work consisted of seven books compiled by Mencius with his disciples including Wan Chang. The discrepancy between this and the Han catalogue listing eleven books suggests either different editorial divisions or possible later additions.
  
  Early Han scholars like Han Ying and Tung Chung-shoo (c. 179-104 BC) frequently quoted from Mencius, indicating its availability and scholarly engagement. Yang Heung (c. 53 BC-18 AD) wrote a commentary that existed through the Sung dynasty, while Wang Ch'ung (c. 27-100 AD) left critical "animadversions" on Mencian philosophy.
  
  The work's preservation through political turmoil and its temporary inclusion in the imperial curriculum demonstrate its growing importance in classical Chinese philosophy during the formative Han period.

  ## 中文
  
  《孟子》的文本历史揭示了其编纂与早期接受的重要细节。根据司马迁记载，原著由孟子与万章等弟子共同编纂，共七篇。这与汉代目录记载十一篇的差异可能源于不同的编辑划分或后世增补。
  
  汉代早期学者如韩婴、董仲舒（约公元前179-104年）频繁引用《孟子》，表明该文本的可得性与学术参与。扬雄（约公元前53年-公元18年）曾撰注释，存世至宋代，而王充（约公元27-100年）则留下对孟子哲学的批判性论述。
  
  该著作在政治动荡中的保存及其在皇家课程中的暂时纳入，证明了其在汉代形成期中国古典哲学中日益增长的重要性。
