## English

**Strategic Planning (畫策)** is the title and central theme of Chapter 18 in *The Book of Lord Shang*. The chapter discusses the fundamental principles that enable a ruler to achieve supremacy: controlling the people, establishing laws, and maintaining military superiority.

### Core Thesis: Control the People to Control the World

The chapter opens with a historical survey showing how the nature of governance must change with circumstances:

- **昊英之世**: In early times, when people were few and beasts were many, people could survive by cutting wood and hunting
- **黄帝之世**: Later, with population growth,黄帝 established social hierarchies and institutions
- **神農之世**: Agricultural society emerged, requiring different governance
- **Later periods**: With "the strong defeating the weak and the many oppressing the few," military power became necessary

The conclusion: "Therefore, using war to end war, though war, is acceptable; using killing to end killing, though killing, is acceptable; using punishment to end punishment, though severe punishment, is acceptable."

### The People as the Foundation

The chapter emphasizes that the people are the foundation of state power:

- "Those who could control the world must first control their people"
- "Those who could defeat strong enemies must first defeat their people"
- The relationship between ruler and people is compared to a metallurgist and metal, or a potter and clay

If the foundation is not firm, the people are like flying birds and running beasts—who can control them?

### Law as the Instrument of Control

The chapter advocates using law (法) to restrain the people:

- "Those who excel at governance block the people with law"
- When law is clear and commands are enforced, "the worthy dare not scheme, the courageous dare not kill, even if the population reaches billions"

### Military Supremacy

The chapter discusses how to create a martial society:

1. **Make the people view war as desirable**: "When the people see battle, they should be like hungry wolves seeing meat"
2. **Severe discipline**: "Fathers send sons, older brothers send younger brothers, wives send husbands—all say: 'If you don't return, I'd rather you die'"
3. **Strict enforcement**: Military regulations must be absolute; those who flee have nowhere to go

The result: "The three armies, following orders like a flowing river, will die rather than retreat."

### The Problem of Law Enforcement

A significant portion of the chapter addresses why laws often fail:

- "Every state has laws, but no law that ensures laws are followed"
- "Every state has laws against奸邪 and盗贼, but no law that ensures they are caught"
- When criminals are not caught, it proves the punishment is too light

The solution: "Those who excel at governance punish the wicked and do not reward the good... Therefore, without punishment, the people are good. Without punishment, the people are good because the punishment is severe."

### The Ideal Ruler

The chapter concludes by describing the ideal ruler:

- The ruler does not need exceptional virtue or wisdom
- The ruler simply needs to ensure laws are clear and enforced
- "The sage knows the necessary principles and acts according to the circumstances"
- "Therefore, governing the people is like controlling water with height, or controlling fire with dryness"

This is summarized in the famous phrase: "The benevolent can be benevolent to others but cannot make others benevolent; the righteous can love others but cannot make others love. Thus, I know that benevolence and righteousness are insufficient to govern the world."

## 中文

**畫策**是《商君書》第十八章的篇名，意為宏觀的戰略規劃。本章論述了君主成就霸業的根本原則：制服民眾、確立法治、保持軍事優勢。

### 核心論點：控制民眾以控制天下

本章以歷史考察開篇，說明治理方式必須隨時勢而變：

- **昊英之世**：早期人口稀少、禽獸繁多，人民以伐木殺獸為生
- **黃帝之世**：人口增長後，黃帝建立君臣上下之義、父子兄弟之禮
- **神農之世**：農業社會出現，需要不同的治理方式
- **後世**：強勝弱、眾暴寡，軍事力量成為必要

結論：「故以戰去戰，雖戰可也；以殺去殺，雖殺可也；以刑去刑，雖重刑可也。」

### 民眾是根本

本章強調民眾是國力的根本：

- 「昔之能制天下者，必先制其民者也」
- 「能勝彊敵者，必先勝其民者也」
- 君民關係比喻為冶金者與金、陶者與土

「本不堅，則民如飛鳥走獸，其孰能制之？」

### 法治是工具

本章主張以法治民：

- 「善治者，塞民以法」
- 當法明令行時，「聖知弗敢我謀，勇力弗敢我殺；雖民至億萬之數」，不敢犯上

### 軍事優先

本章論述如何建立軍事化社會：

1. **使民樂戰**：「民之見戰也，如餓狼之見肉」
2. **嚴格紀律**：「父遺其子，兄遺其弟，妻遺其夫，皆曰：不得，無返」
3. **令行禁止**：「行間無所逃，遷徙無所入」

結果：「三軍之眾，從令如流，死而不旋踵。」

### 法治的困境

本章相當篇幅探討法律何以失效：

- 「國皆有法，而無使法必行之法」
- 「國皆有禁姦邪刑盜賊之法，而無使姦邪盜賊必得之法」

解決方案：「善治者，刑不善，而不賞善，故不刑而民善。不刑而民善，刑重也。」

### 理想君主

本章最後描述理想君主：

- 君主無需聖德異才
- 只需確保「法必明，令必行」
- 「聖人知必然之理，必為之時勢」
- 「故其制民也，如以高下制水，如以燥溼制火」

總結名言：「仁者能仁於人，而不能使人仁；義者能愛於人，而不能使人愛。是以知仁義之不足以治天下也。」

## 日本語

**畫策**（かくさく）は『商君書』第十八章の篇名であり、宏观的な戦略計画を指す。本章では君主が霸業成就のための根本原則—民衆の制御、法の確立、軍事的優位の維持—を論じている。

### 核心論点：民衆を制して天下を制す

本章は歴史的考察から始まり、治理方法が時勢とともに変化しなければならないことを示す：
- **昊英の世**：早期は人口稀少で獣が多かった
- **黄帝の世**：人口増加後、黄帝は社会秩序を確立
- **神農の世**：農業社会が出現
- **後世**：強者が弱者を制し、多数が少数を圧迫

結論：「以戦去戦、以殺去殺、以刑去刑」という変容の論理。

### 民衆は根本

本章は民衆が国力の根本であることを強調：
- 「天下を制する者は必ず 먼저民を制す」
- 「強敵に勝てる者は必ず 먼저民に勝てる」
- 君民の関係は冶金者と金、陶工と土に例えられる

### 法治理論

本章は法による民衆統制を主張：
- 「善く治める者は法によって民を塞ぐ」
- 法が明確で命令が執行されれば、「聖知も謀る胆勇力も殺す胆敢がない」

### 軍事優先

軍事化社会の作り方を論じる：
1. **民に戦を好ませる**：「民が戦を見ると、餓狼が肉を見るようだ」
2. **厳格な規律**：「父は子に、兄は弟に、妻は夫に、『帰らぬなら死ね』」
3. **厳格な執行**：「逃亡する場所がない」

結果：「三軍は命令に従い流れのように、死しても振り返らない」

### 法執行の問題

なぜ法律が失敗するかを大幅に論じる：
- 「国には法があるが、法を必ず実行させる法がない」
- 「姦邪・盗賊を禁じる法があるが、捕まえる法がない」

解決策：「善い者を賞せず、悪い者を罰する...罰が重ければ、民は罰なしでも善くなる」
