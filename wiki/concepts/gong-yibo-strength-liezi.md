## English

The anecdote of **Gong Yibo** (公儀伯) in the *Liezi* chapter "Zhongni" presents a Daoist teaching about the relationship between true strength and non-action, through a dialogue with King Xuan of Zhou.

**The setting**: Gong Yibo was renowned throughout the feudal lords for his strength. The Zhou King Xuan, informed by Lord Tangxi (堂谿公), summoned Gong Yibo with full ceremonial honors.

**The paradox**: Upon meeting Gong Yibo, the king found him to appear weak and timid. Puzzled, the king asked about the nature of his strength.
