## English

The Daxizhuan (大傳, "Great Treatise") chapter provides the foundational description of the Chinese **lineage system** (宗法制度), the complex kinship organization that structured aristocratic society throughout the Zhou dynasty and beyond. This system determined inheritance rights, mourning obligations, and the entire framework of ancestral veneration.

### Core Principle: The Separate Branch

The central doctrine states: "別子為祖，繼別為宗，繼禰者為小宗" (The separate branch becomes the ancestor; the one who inherits from the separate branch becomes the clan head; the one who inherits from the father becomes the minor lineage head).

A **別子 (biézǐ)** was a son who did not inherit the father's position—typically a younger son of a noble who left to establish a new line. This separate line then traced its founding ancestor to this biézǐ, creating a distinct lineage branch. The eldest son who did inherit was called the **大宗 (dàzōng)**—the "great lineage"—while subsequent generations became **小宗 (xiǎozōng)**—"minor lineages."

### Perpetuity and Transience

The Daxizhuan distinguishes two types of lineage heads:

- **百世不遷之宗**: A lineage that never migrates or loses its identity—a lineage tracing back to a biézǐ, the founder of a new line.
- **五世則遷之宗**: A lineage that changes after five generations, when ancestral ties to more distant ancestors are severed for mourning purposes.

The great lineage (大宗) is permanent; the minor lineages (小宗) change focus as one moves up the generational tree: "自仁率親，等而上之，至於祖" (Following parental affection upward to the ancestor). This creates a system where every individual belongs to both a great lineage (connecting to the founding ancestor) and multiple minor lineages (connecting to more recent ancestors at different generational removes).

### Implications: Ancestor Worship and Social Cohesion

The text explicitly connects lineage organization to the entire Confucian ritual and moral order:

> "尊祖故敬宗。敬宗，尊祖之義也。"

(Revering ancestors, one respects the lineage head; respecting the lineage head is the meaning of revering ancestors.)

This chain of causation leads through **收族** (uniting the clan), **宗廟嚴** (maintaining solemn ancestral temples), **社稷重** (venerating the state altars), **愛百姓** (loving the people), **刑罰中** (administering just punishment), and ultimately to **樂** (joy/harmony).

### Mourning Grades and Lineage Distance

The Daxizhuan specifies mourning grades based on lineage proximity:

- **四世而緦**: Fourth-generation relatives wear the coarsest hemp (si cloth)—the limit of mourning obligation.
- **五世袒免**: Fifth-generation relatives wear only white robes and cap (tan mian)—severely reduced mourning.
- **六世，親屬竭矣**: Sixth generation severs all mourning connections—relatives no longer belong to the same kinship category.

### The Principle of Same-Surname Exclusivity

"同姓從宗，合族屬；異姓主名，治際會。名著而男女有別。" establishes that same-surname individuals follow their lineage organization, while those of different surnames are identified by their role (mother, wife, daughter-in-law) based on their husband's position. The emphasis on **男女有別** (gender distinction) reflects the Confucian concern with maintaining proper sexual differentiation in ritual and social life.

## 中文

《禮記·大傳》系統闡述宗法制度，此乃華夏古代社會血緣組織之根本大法，決定了祭祀、喪服、繼承之各項秩序。

### 別子為祖

宗法之核心原則為「別子為祖，繼別為宗，繼禰者為小宗」。

別子者，諸侯、卿大夫之庶子，不襲父位者也。別子離開本宗，另立門戶，其子孫遂奉別子為始祖。別子之嫡長子，繼續別子之祭祀，是為大宗；其餘子孫，則各為小宗。

### 永傳與五世則遷

大宗者，百世不遷。蓋大宗傳自別子，永守其祖之祭祀，綿延不絕。

小宗者，五世則遷。蓋小宗之祖，止及高祖；五世之後，則遷其宗，另奉高祖為祖。此所以《大傳》云：「宗其繼別子者，百世不遷者也；宗其繼高祖者，五世則遷者也。」

### 尊祖敬宗之義

《大傳》以為，尊祖故敬宗，敬宗故收族，收族故宗廟嚴，宗廟嚴故重社稷，重社稷故愛百姓，愛百姓故刑罰中，刑罰中故庶民安，庶民安故財用足，財用足故百志成，百志成故禮俗刑，禮俗刑然後樂。此一貫串之政治哲學，由宗法而達天下太平。

### 喪服制度

《大傳》詳論喪服與宗族遠近之關係：

- 四世而緦：四代以內，血緣尚近，當為之服緦麻。
- 五世袒免：五代以外，恩情漸薄，但為之袒免。
- 六世親屬竭：六代而後，恩義斷絕，不復為親屬。

### 同姓異姓之別

同姓者從宗，合族屬；異姓者主名，治際會。蓋異姓女子入嫁，須以夫之倫類定其名分。此所以別男女、明夫婦之道也。

## 日本語

『禮記·大傳』は中國古代の宗法制度を体系的に阐述し、周代貴族社会の血縁組織と祭祀秩序の根本原理を明らかにする。

### 別子為祖の原則

宗法制度の核心は「別子為祖、繼別為宗、繼禰者為小宗」である。

別子とは、諸侯や卿大夫の庶子で、父の地位を継承せず、新たな系統を立てた者である。別子の子がその祭祀を継承し、これが大宗となる。其他の子孙は小宗となる。

### 永続と五世則遷

大宗は百世不遷である。別子から続く系統は永久に変わらない。

小宗は五世則遷である。高祖を超えて祖先祭祀は行わず、五世ごとに祭祀の焦点が移る。

### 尊祖敬宗の思想

大伝は、「尊祖故敬宗、敬宗故收族、收族故宗廟嚴、宗廟嚴故重社稷、重社稷故愛百姓、愛百姓故刑罰中、刑罰中故庶民安、庶民安故財用足、財用足故百志成、百志成故禮俗刑、禮俗刑然後楽」と論じる。これは宗法から天下太平への一貫した政治哲学を示す。
