## English

The concept of "Three Years Mourning" refers to the traditional practice within Confucian ethics, highlighting the importance of filial piety (xiao) and respect towards deceased parents. According to Mencius, this mourning period is not merely a formality; it is a deep expression of loyalty and love toward one's parents that results in a collective societal understanding of familial bonds and responsibilities.

During this period, individuals are expected to refrain from public festivities and maintain a demeanor of sadness as a sign of respect for the loss. Mencius articulated that the proper observance of mourning is crucial for instilling moral values and sustaining social order. It reinforces the belief that familial relationships and responsibilities are fundamental to societal harmony.

The expectation of three years of mourning serves to cultivate deeper emotional bonds within families and between generations, fostering a culture of care and responsibility. The underlying principle is that respect and remembrance for the deceased govern future actions, thus extending the impact of one's parents' teachings throughout the lives of their children.

在此期间，个人被期望避免公共的庆祝活动，保持悲伤的表现，以作为对失去亲人的尊敬。孟子指出，正确遵守哀悼是灌输道德价值观和维持社会秩序的重要因素。它强化了家庭关系和责任在社会和谐中根本的重要性。

三年的哀悼期旨在培养家庭内部以及代际之间更深厚的情感纽带，促进关心和责任感的文化。其根本原则是尊重和纪念已故亲人对未来行为的指导，从而延续父母教诲对孩子一生的影响。

## English
The **Three Years Mourning** is a Confucian ritual practice requiring children to mourn their parents for three years after their death. This practice demonstrates the depth of [[filial-piety]] in [[Confucianism]].

In the [[confucius-analects]] "Xue Er" chapter, Confucius states: "When his father is alive, observe his intentions; when his father is dead, observe his conduct. If for three years he does not alter from the way of his father, he may be called filial." This emphasizes that true filial piety extends beyond mere ritual observance to embodying the moral way of one's parents.

The three-year period symbolizes the length of time parents cared for their children during infancy, creating a reciprocal relationship of care and respect. This practice reinforces the importance of family continuity and moral inheritance in Confucian social ethics.

## 中文
**三年之丧**是儒家丧礼制度，要求子女为父母守丧三年。这一实践体现了儒家思想中[[孝道]]的深刻内涵。

在《[[confucius-analects|论语]]·学而》篇中，孔子提出："父在，观其志；父没，观其行；三年无改于父之道，可谓孝矣。"这强调真正的孝道超越仪式性表现，而在于继承父母的道德之道。

三年守丧期象征父母养育幼儿所需的时间，建立了照顾与尊重的互惠关系。这一制度强化了儒家社会伦理中家庭延续与道德传承的重要性。

三年間の喪期間は、父母が幼児期に子女を養育した期間を象徴し、ケアと尊重の相互関係を創出します。この慣行は儒教的社会倫理における家族の継続性と道徳的継承の重要性を強化します。
