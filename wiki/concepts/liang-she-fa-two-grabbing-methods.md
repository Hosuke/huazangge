## English

The "Two Grabbing Methods" (二攝法, also translated as "Two Embracing Methods" or "Two Methods of Grasping") refers to the dual methodology employed by bodhisattvas to benefit all sentient beings. This doctrine, found in the *Bodhisattva Origin Stories* (菩薩本緣經), presents the complementary approach of providing material support alongside spiritual teaching.

### The Two Methods in Practice

According to the text, the bodhisattva Vīrāma (毘羅摩) employed this dual strategy to greatest effect:

1. **Material Provision (財施)**: The first method involves providing physical necessities—food, clothing, shelter, medicine, vehicles, wealth, and other material goods. This addresses the immediate suffering and needs of beings, freeing them from distress so they can receive teachings.

2. **Dharma Teaching (法施)**: The second method involves sharing the profound teachings of Buddhism—explaining the nature of impermanence, the suffering of cyclic existence, and the path to liberation. This addresses the deeper spiritual needs of beings.

### The Rationale: Meeting Beings Where They Are

The text explains that sentient beings possess diverse dispositions and needs. Some are drawn to hearing the Dharma, others crave wealth and material goods, some delight in pleasant speech, others prefer solitude with the virtuous, while still others are attached to worldly pleasures and never tire of acquiring more. No single approach can reach all beings effectively.

Vīrāma therefore adopted the method of first satisfying beings' physical needs, creating the conditions for them to accept spiritual teachings:

> "Fortunate indeed that I possess wise and skillful methods, capable of gathering all beings and establishing them in the unsurpassed, anuttara-samyak-sambodhi. I also possess another method. Just as the sun, though able to illuminate the entire world, cannot become light for the blind; so I, though able to proclaim the unsurpassed path for all beings, cannot bring benefit to those without eyes of wisdom. Therefore I shall now provide them with clothing, food, and drink until they are satisfied and joyful, and only then shall I teach them the Dharma, causing them to believe and accept."

### The Bodhisattva's Compassionate Motivation

The Two Methods emerge from the bodhisattva's fundamental compassion. Vīrāma reflects:

> "When I reflect on this matter, I consider how I might benefit the king's body and all the people of his kingdom, sparing them from all suffering. I also contemplate the merits and faults of both king and people. If one first practices goodness and then practices evil, this does not constitute being human. Better to speak truthfully and incur enmity than to speak flatteringly and gain intimacy. Better to proclaim the correct Dharma and fall into hell than to speak heterodox words and be born in heaven."

### Connection to the Six Perfections

The Two Grabbing Methods represent the harmonization of the perfection of giving (檀波羅蜜, dāna-pāramitā) with skillful means (方便, upāya). The bodhisattva does not merely give material things, nor merely give teachings—both must be integrated appropriately to the capacity and needs of each being.

### Related Concepts

The Two Grabbing Methods relate to [[dana-paramita-bodhisattva-giving|the perfection of giving]], [[upaya-expedient-means-buddhism|skillful means]], and the bodhisattva's [[bodhisattva-path|path of cultivation]]. This methodology reflects the Mahāyāna understanding that beings must first be freed from material suffering before they can aspire to spiritual liberation.

## 中文

二攝法，又稱「二攝」或「二種攝取法門」，乃大乘佛教中闡述菩薩度化眾生之根本方法。此法見於《菩薩本緣經》，具體指以財物救濟與法語開示雙軌並進之教化策略。

### 財法二攝之義

據經文所載，毘羅摩菩薩稟持此二門攝化之法：

一者、**財施**：救護眾生之肉身，先以衣服、飲食、醫藥、臥具、車乘、珍寶等資生之物隨意給施，令其免於匮乏之苦。經云：「我今當以衣服、飲食而給足之，令其飽滿，心歡喜已，然後復當為之說法。」此乃先以財施滿足眾生現世之需，令其心安道隆。

二者、**法施**：繼以正法開示，演說無常、苦、空、無我之理，令眾生的了悟生死流轉之真相，發心趣向無上菩提。經云：「汝等今已受我供奉，好自利益當觀正法，若死至時雖有父母、妻子、親族無量財寶，不能令命住一念頃。」此乃以法施究竟度化眾生之慧命。

### 隨機設化之要

《菩薩本緣經》詳述眾生根器各異、貪欲不同：「然諸眾生受性不同，或欲聞法、或貪財貨、或嗜五欲、或樂愛語、或好憤鬧多人親附、或好隨逐善人之行、或樂多愛心無厭足。」面對如此多樣之眾生機宜，單一法門難以普被一切。

故毘羅摩菩薩思惟：「我今幸有大智方便，悉能攝取一切眾生，安止住於阿耨多羅三藐三菩提，我亦復有餘方便。譬如日出，雖能照了一切天下，然不能為盲者作明；我亦如是，雖復能為一切眾生說無上道，然不能為無慧目者而作利益。」此即揭示：唯有先以外財資其色身，方能進以正法開其慧目。

### 檀波羅蜜之究竟

二攝法者，實乃[[dana-paramita-bodhisattva-giving|檀波羅蜜]]之圓滿修學。經云：「是毘羅摩菩薩摩訶薩，以二攝法攝取眾生，所謂財法。」然菩薩行布施時，不以財物為究竟，必以法施為指歸，令眾生「悉得如來七菩提寶」，究竟斷除貪欲，得證無上解脫。

### 相關義理

此二攝法與大乘[[upaya-expedient-means-buddhism|方便]]思想相通，顯示[[bodhisattva-path|菩薩道]]中悲智雙運之要旨。菩薩本願在於「拔濟眾生永離老死」，非僅救一時之困厄，實欲令一切有情究竟離苦得樂。

## 日本語

二攝法（にしょうかぶし）、または「二つの摂取法門」とは、大乗仏教における菩薩の眾生救済の基本的方法論である。『菩薩本縁経』に基づき、物質的な施しと仏法の教授という二重の手法を統合する教えである。

### 二法の具体的內容

経典によると、毘羅摩菩萨は以下の二つの方法を並行して用いた：

一、**財施（ざいせ）**：衣服・飲食・醫薬・臥具・車乘・寶物などの生活必需品を眾生に与え、まず物質的な困窮から解放する。『経』에는：「我れ今當に衣服・飲食を以て之が足らしめん、使其飽滿心喜び已りて、然る後復た當に之が爲に法を說かん」

二、**法施（ぼっせ）**：次に无常・苦・空・無我の真理を説き、眾生の生死流転の本質を悟らせ、無上菩提への道を開示する。『経』에는：「汝等今已に私の供奉を受け、好んで自利し當に正法を觀ずべし」

### 眾生の機根に應じた化導

経典は眾生の多様性を具体的に記述する：「諸眾生は受性各異、或は法を聞き、或は財貨を貪り、或は五欲を嗜み、或は美言を樂み、或は善人に隨いて行爲したがる」このような複雑な眾生の機根に対して、单一な方法では十分な救済是不可能的である。

毘羅摩菩萨の智慧は于此に表れる：「日出づるが如く、天下を照らすこと能的だが、盲目者のために明となることはできない; 我も亦無上道を說くこと能的だが、智慧の目なき者のために利益となることはできない」

### 檀波羅蜜との関連

二攝法は[[dana-paramita-bodhisattva-giving|檀波羅蜜]]の完遂であり、[[upaya-expedient-means-buddhism|方便]]智慧の具體的表れである。物質的施しと精神的教授を組み合わせることにより、菩薩は一切眾生を一時的痛苦と永遠の生死から同時に救済することができる。
